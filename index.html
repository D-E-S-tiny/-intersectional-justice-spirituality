Intersectional Justice Spirituality

Embracing justice that honors the full complexity of identity, oppression, and the Earth

🌍 Our Mission

To cultivate a spiritual practice rooted in radical liberation, ecological reverence, and sacred solidarity — one that recognizes that true justice must include the land, the waters, and all living beings.

📜 What We Believe

  • 🌱 Ecological responsibility
  • 🤝 Collective healing
  • 🧠 Critical reflection
  • ✊ Resistance to systemic oppression

🛠️ Systems We Confront

1. Capitalism 🟥

Root of exploitation. Liberation = worker power, land return, community wealth.

2. White Supremacy & Imperialism 🤍

Global racial control. Liberation = Indigenous sovereignty, migrant justice.

3. Patriarchy & Heteronormativity 🏳️‍🌈

Enforces binaries. Liberation = queer love, gender freedom, reproductive rights.

4. Ecocide 🌱

Profits from Earth’s destruction. Liberation = climate justice, land back, eco-socialism.

5. Ableism ♿

Devalues disabled lives. Liberation = disability justice, care economies, rest.
